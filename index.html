Custom Ride

Get inspired Real people, real experiences

The Tuk Tuk tour of Lisbon, as guided by Luciano, was one of the best tours we’ve ever taken — and we’ve taken plenty of tours in Europe, Australia, New Zealand, and the USA.
This is an excellent way to start your Lisbon experience. You determine what you want to see and how long you want the tour to last. Initially, we thought a 90-minute tour would be sufficient, but the more we saw, the longer we wanted the tour. We ended up going for 3 1/2 hours! Afterward, we had seen the majority of important Lisbon sites.
Luciano is the ideal tour guide. He’s a gentleman, for starters — he was always ready to help out my husband, who is experiencing mobility issues. Luciano is also warm, and friendly, has a good sense of humor, and is highly knowledgeable about history. I can’t imagine a better tour guide.
The streets of Alfama, from where our tour began and ended, are cobblestone and filled with potholes, and as in any Tuk Tuk, you will likely feel every bump. Also, I suspect you can find less expensive Tuk Tuk tours — but I doubt you’d find a more satisfying, memorable one!
I rarely rate anything five stars, but in the case of Tagus Tours and Luciano, I’m happy to give the maximum star rating.
